  <h1> Classic Choclate Chip Cookies</h1>

  <p>These soft and chewy chocolate chip cookies are a timeless favorite that's perfect for any occasion. Ready in just 30 minutes!</p>

  

  <h2>Ingredients</h2>

  <ul>

      <li> 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour</li>

      <li> 1 cup butter, softened</li>

      <li> 3/4 cup granulated sugar</li>

      <li> 2 large eggs</li>

      <li> 2 cups choclate chips</li>

      <li> 1 teaspoon vanilla extract</li>

      <li> 1 teaspoon baking soda</li>

      <li> 1/2 teasoon salt</li>

  </ul>

  

  <h2> Instructions</h2>

  

  <ol>

         <li>Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).</li>

        <li>Mix the butter and sugars together until creamy, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.</li>

        <li>In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt, then gradually blend into the butter mixture.</li>

        <li>Stir in the chocolate chips by hand.</li>

        <li>Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased cookie sheets.</li>

        <li>Bake for 9-11 minutes or until golden brown.</li>

        <li>Let cool on the baking sheet for 2 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.</li>

    </ol>
